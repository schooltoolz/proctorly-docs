Every proctored exam in Proctorly starts with a test. Creating one takes under two minutes: give the test a title, point Proctorly at your exam content, and click **Create test**. Proctorly generates a unique secure token for the test, and the student access URL — `proctorly.schooltoolz.com/t/<token>` — is ready to share immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the new test form">
    Sign in at [proctorly.schooltoolz.com](https://proctorly.schooltoolz.com) and click **New test** from your dashboard or the **Tests** page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter a test title">
    Type a descriptive title such as `Midterm Exam — Spring 2026`. The title appears in your dashboard, in the live monitoring view, and in the student invitation email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your exam source">
    Select either **Google Forms URL** or **Upload file** using the toggle at the top of the form.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Google Forms URL">
        Paste the full URL of your Google Form. The URL must contain `docs.google.com/forms` — for example:

        ```text
        https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1AbCdEfGhIjKlMnOpQrStUvWx/viewform
        ```

        Proctorly embeds the form inside the student's proctored session. Students submit answers directly through Google Forms while Proctorly monitors their browser in the background.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Upload file">
        Click the upload area or drag and drop a file. Accepted formats:

        | Format | Extension | Notes |
        |--------|-----------|-------|
        | PDF | `.pdf` | Displayed as a read-only document |
        | Excel | `.xlsx` | Rendered as an HTML table |
        | Word | `.docx` | Rendered as formatted HTML |

        Maximum file size is **10 MB**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create the test">
    Click **Create test**. Proctorly returns a shareable link — for example:

    ```text
    https://proctorly.schooltoolz.com/t/a1b2c3d4e5f6
    ```

    Click **Copy link** to copy it to your clipboard. You can share this link directly, or use Proctorly's built-in invitation flow to send personalized links to each student by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plan limits

The number of tests you can create per month and the number of students allowed per test depends on your plan.

| Plan | Tests per month | Students per test | Report retention |
|------|----------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Free | 2 | 15 | 7 days |
| Classroom | Unlimited | 30 | 90 days |
| School | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

<Note>
  School plan includes up to 5 teacher seats. Upgrade your plan from the **Billing** page.
</Note>

If you reach your monthly test limit or your per-test student cap, Proctorly shows an error when you try to proceed.
