The live monitoring dashboard gives you a real-time view of every student session while the exam is in progress. Open it from the test detail page by clicking **Live monitor**. The dashboard updates automatically — you do not need to refresh. Each session row shows the student's name, current flag count, and a color-coded risk level: green for Low, amber for Medium, and red for High.

## What you see

Sessions are listed in the active sessions table. Each row shows:

- Student name
- Number of flags raised so far
- Risk level (Low / Medium / High) with color coding
- Time elapsed
- Any pending pause requests

A summary bar at the top shows the total count of active sessions at a glance.

## Broadcasting messages to students

You can send a text message to one student or all active students directly from the live dashboard. Messages are delivered as a banner notification inside the student's exam window.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the message panel">
    Click **Broadcast** in the dashboard toolbar to open the message panel. To message a single student, click the message icon on their session row instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your message">
    Type your message in the text field. Messages must be between **1 and 280 characters**. A character counter is displayed below the field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send to all** to broadcast to every active session, or **Send** to deliver to the selected student. The message appears immediately in the student's exam window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Approving and rejecting pause requests

When a student clicks the pause button, a notification appears on your dashboard. The notification shows the student's name, their optional pause note, and the time elapsed since they requested the pause.

Click **Approve** to pause the student's session. Click **Reject** to dismiss the request and let their timer continue. Once approved, click **Resume** on the student's row to end the pause and return them to active status.

## Mass controls

The mass controls bar at the top of the dashboard applies an action to all active sessions at once. Use these when you need to address the whole class simultaneously.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Mass screenshot" icon="camera">
    Captures a webcam snapshot from every active session immediately. Snapshots appear in each student's session gallery in the post-exam report.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mass pause / resume" icon="pause">
    Pauses all active sessions at once, stopping every student's timer. Click again to resume everyone. A confirmation prompt appears before either action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mass lock / unlock" icon="lock">
    Locks all screens, preventing students from interacting with their exam. Use this if you need to address the class without students continuing. A confirmation prompt appears before locking.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Mass pause and mass lock affect every active session for the test. Students see a notification on their screen when these actions are applied. Use them deliberately.
</Warning>
