When something goes wrong during setup or an exam, most issues fall into one of three categories: camera and screen share permissions blocked by the browser, students unable to access or complete an exam, and monitoring flags that need context or review.

The guides in this section walk you through each category with specific steps. If your issue is not covered here, contact support at [support@proctorly.schooltoolz.com](mailto:support@proctorly.schooltoolz.com).

<Note>
  Before digging into a specific guide, confirm which browser the student or teacher is using. Chrome or Edge are required for full functionality. Firefox has limited screen share API support and is not recommended.
</Note>

## In this section

- **[Camera and screen share permissions](/troubleshooting/camera-and-permissions)** — Unblock camera access in Chrome and Edge, fix MediaPipe load failures, and resolve wrong-camera selections.
- **[Exam link and access problems](/troubleshooting/exam-access)** — Fix invalid or expired tokens, blank exam screens, wrong email entries, and paused or locked exams.
- **[Monitoring flags](/troubleshooting/monitoring-flags)** — Understand what each flag type means, why false positives occur, and how to dismiss flags or adjust sensitivity.
