Billing runs through Stripe, and you manage everything from the **Billing** screen in your dashboard. The answers below cover what's confirmed about how plans and billing work. Anything not yet settled is flagged so it isn't guessed at.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What plans are available?">
    Three tiers: **Free**, **Classroom**, and **School**. Every account starts on Free. Classroom and School can be billed **monthly or annually**. See [Plans](/billing/plans) for what each one unlocks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or change my plan?">
    Open the **Billing** screen, choose the plan and billing interval you want, and complete the change through **Stripe Checkout**. The current price is shown live before you confirm.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch between monthly and annual?">
    Yes. You can toggle between monthly and annual billing from the **Billing** screen.

    <Info>
      **Reviewer TODO.** Whether switching is prorated, and exactly when the new interval takes effect, isn't documented in the product. Confirm with the Proctorly team before describing the proration behavior.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel?">
    You can cancel your subscription from the **Billing** screen.

    <Info>
      **Reviewer TODO.** What happens to your account and existing reports after cancellation (immediate downgrade vs. end-of-period, and data retention) isn't settled. Don't publish specifics until confirmed.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Integrity Pass?">
    It's a separate add-on, purchased independently of your plan. What it includes isn't finalized yet — see [Add-ons](/billing/credits).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
