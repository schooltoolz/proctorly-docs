Proctorly billing is managed through Stripe. You can subscribe to a monthly or annual plan, purchase one-time credit bundles, or add the Integrity Pass subscription to cover Full Sweep AI proctoring on every exam. All payment and subscription management happens in the Stripe billing portal, accessible from **Settings → Billing** inside your Proctorly account.

The articles in this section explain the three subscription plans in detail, how the credits system works, and answers to common billing questions.
