Proctorly is a paid product with three plan tiers — **Free**, **Classroom**, and **School** — plus an optional **Integrity Pass** add-on. Billing runs through Stripe, and you manage your plan, billing interval, and add-on from the **Billing** screen in your dashboard.

<Info>
  Prices aren't listed in these docs. Proctorly shows live prices from Stripe at checkout, so the **Billing** screen is always the accurate place to check the current cost.
</Info>
