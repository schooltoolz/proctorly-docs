Proctorly has three plan tiers — **Free**, **Classroom**, and **School**. Every account starts on Free, and you can move between plans at any time. Billing runs through Stripe, so the current price for each plan is always shown live on the **Billing** screen rather than fixed in this guide.

<Info>
  **Prices aren't listed here on purpose.** Proctorly renders live prices from Stripe at checkout. Always check the **Billing** screen for the current monthly and annual amounts — they are the source of truth.
</Info>

## What each plan unlocks

The tiers differ mainly in how many tests you can run, how many students each test allows, and how snapshots are stored during proctoring.

| What you get | Free | Classroom | School |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly test limit** | Limited (trial cap) | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Students per test** | Limited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Billing options** | — | Monthly or annual | Monthly or annual |
| **Snapshot capture** | Full | Selective | Full |

## Snapshot capture: Full vs Selective

Every plan proctors the same set of integrity signals. The difference is which **snapshots** (webcam and screen images) get stored for later review:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Full capture" icon="images">
    Keeps a snapshot for every flag type, plus the regular baseline snapshots. Available on **Free** and **School**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Selective capture" icon="filter">
    Still proctors everything, but only stores snapshots for the most important events — critical or high-confidence flags. Available on **Classroom**. This keeps storage lean without changing what's monitored.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

In short: **Classroom keeps snapshots for the most important flags; School keeps them all.** What Proctorly *watches for* is the same on every plan — only what it *stores* differs.

## Changing your plan

You manage your subscription from the **Billing** screen inside your dashboard. From there you can:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing">
    Go to the **Billing** area of your Proctorly dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan and interval">
    Pick **Classroom** or **School**, and choose **monthly** or **annual** billing. The current price is shown live before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out through Stripe">
    Proctorly hands you off to **Stripe Checkout** to enter payment details and complete the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can switch plans, change between monthly and annual, or cancel from the same screen. See the [Billing FAQ](/billing/faq) for what happens when you change or cancel.
