Proctorly offers three subscription tiers — Free, Classroom, and School — each designed for a different scale of use. Every account starts on the Free plan automatically; no credit card is required. You can upgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing → Upgrade**.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Free | Classroom | School |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly price** | $0 | $19/mo | $49/mo |
| **Annual price** | — | $144/yr ($12/mo) | $348/yr ($29/mo) |
| **Tests per month** | 2 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Students per test** | 15 | 30 | Unlimited |
| **Teacher seats** | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| **Report retention** | 7 days | 90 days | Unlimited |

## What each limit means in practice

**Tests per month** counts the number of distinct tests that receive at least one student session in a calendar month. Creating a test does not consume your quota — a test only counts once a student joins and begins their session.

**Students per test** is the maximum number of student sessions for a single test. If you reach the limit on a test, new students who open the exam link will see a message that the session is full. You can raise this limit by upgrading your plan.

**Teacher seats** controls how many educator accounts can be members of the same Proctorly workspace. On Free and Classroom, only one teacher can access the workspace. School allows up to five teacher seats, making it suitable for departments where multiple instructors need to create and review exams independently.

**Report retention** determines how long Proctorly keeps completed session reports — including webcam snapshots, flag timelines, and AI summaries. On the Free plan, reports are automatically deleted after seven days. On Classroom they are retained for 90 days, and on School they are kept indefinitely.

## Annual billing savings

Paying annually locks in a lower effective monthly rate and is billed as a single upfront charge.

- **Classroom annual** — $144/yr ($12/mo effective) saves roughly **37%** compared with monthly billing.
- **School annual** — $348/yr ($29/mo effective) saves roughly **41%** compared with monthly billing.

<Tip>
  If you are confident you will use Proctorly throughout an academic year, annual billing is the more economical choice. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time from the billing portal without losing your current billing period.
</Tip>

## How to upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your account avatar in the top-right corner of the Proctorly dashboard and select **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Select the **Billing** tab in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Upgrade">
    Click **Upgrade** next to the plan you want. You are taken to the Stripe billing portal to confirm your payment details and complete the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your new plan limits take effect immediately after the payment is processed. Existing reports and tests are not affected when upgrading.

<Note>
  The Free plan allows 2 tests per month and caps each test at 15 students. If you need to run more tests or a larger class before the end of the billing month, upgrading to Classroom or purchasing a credit bundle to enable Full Sweep on existing tests will not increase those hard caps — you need to upgrade your plan to remove them.
</Note>
