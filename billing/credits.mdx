Alongside the three plans, Proctorly offers an **Integrity Pass** add-on. It's a separate purchase from your main subscription, handled through the same Stripe billing flow on the **Billing** screen.

<Warning>
  **Reviewer TODO — not confirmed in the product.** What the **Integrity Pass** add-on actually includes — and whether it is a one-time purchase, a recurring add-on, or a credit bundle — is **not settled** in the product yet. The details below are intentionally left open. Do not publish specifics (pricing, what it covers, how it's consumed) until a human confirms them. See [Open questions](#open-questions).
</Warning>

## What we can say today

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="It's a separate purchase" icon="cart-shopping">
    The Integrity Pass is bought on its own, independent of whether you're on Free, Classroom, or School.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Managed through Stripe" icon="credit-card">
    Like plan changes, you add or remove it from the **Billing** screen, which hands you off to Stripe.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
