Full Sweep is Proctorly's highest-fidelity AI proctoring mode. When a test runs with Full Sweep enabled, the AI performs deeper behavioral analysis on each student session — this includes continuous gaze tracking, audio anomaly detection, and a more thorough environment scan at session start. Because Full Sweep consumes significantly more compute per session, it draws from a credits balance rather than being included in the base subscription.

## How credits work

One credit is consumed per student exam session when Full Sweep is active on that test. Credits are deducted at the moment a student's session is finalized — not when a student joins or while the exam is in progress. If a student starts but does not complete the exam (for example, they lose connection before submitting), no credit is consumed.

Credits are account-level and shared across all tests in your workspace. They do not expire and they roll over indefinitely.

## Credit bundle pricing

Credit bundles are one-time purchases. You can buy as many bundles as you need at any time from **Settings → Billing**.

| Bundle | Credits | Price | Effective cost per credit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 5 | $5 | $1.00 |
| Standard | 11 | $10 | ~$0.91 |
| Plus | 30 | $25 | ~$0.83 |
| Pro Pack | 65 | $50 | ~$0.77 |

Larger bundles cost less per credit. If you run large cohorts regularly, the Pro Pack offers the best per-session value.

## Integrity Pass: the subscription alternative

Instead of purchasing credits individually, you can subscribe to **Integrity Pass** for **$9/month**. While Integrity Pass is active, Full Sweep is available on every test in your workspace without consuming any credits from your balance.

Integrity Pass is a recurring monthly subscription billed separately from your main plan. You can add or cancel it at any time from the billing portal. Cancelling Integrity Pass does not remove credits you have already purchased — those remain in your balance and will be consumed again if you run Full Sweep tests after the subscription ends.

## When credits are consumed vs. when Integrity Pass covers it

| Scenario | Credits consumed? |
|---|---|
| Full Sweep test, Integrity Pass active | No — Integrity Pass covers it |
| Full Sweep test, no Integrity Pass | Yes — 1 credit per completed session |
| Standard (non-Full Sweep) test, any billing state | No |
| Student joins but does not complete the session | No |

<Tip>
  If you run Full Sweep on more than 9 sessions per month, Integrity Pass ($9/mo) costs less than buying individual credits at the Standard bundle rate (~$0.91/credit). For anything over roughly 10 sessions a month, the subscription pays for itself.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Credits cover Full Sweep only. They do not unlock higher student-per-test limits or additional teacher seats — those are controlled by your subscription plan.
</Warning>
