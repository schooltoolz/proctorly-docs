description: "Learn what to expect as a student in a Proctorly-proctored exam — from clicking your unique link to completing the exam and seeing the done screen."
---

You do not need a Proctorly account to take a proctored exam. Your teacher sends you a unique link, and everything happens in your browser. When you open the link, Proctorly guides you through a short setup to grant camera and screen-share permissions, then you take the exam as normal.

During the session, Proctorly monitors your webcam, the active tab, ambient audio, and your shared screen in the background. You will not see any pop-ups or interruptions while you work unless you request a pause or your browser blocks a required permission. The monitoring engine runs entirely in your browser tab — there is no extension to install and no software to download.

When you submit your exam, the monitoring session ends and you see a completion screen. If your teacher has enabled identity verification, you will be prompted to take a quick photo of your ID before the session closes. After that, you are done — your teacher reviews the session record and any flags on their end.

</Note>
