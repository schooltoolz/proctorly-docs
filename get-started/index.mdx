Proctorly is a browser-based exam proctoring platform built for educators. You create a test, share a unique link with your students, and Proctorly monitors each session automatically — detecting face presence, tab switching, audio, and screen activity — all without any browser extension or native app.

After an exam ends, you review an AI-generated integrity report for each student. Proctorly flags suspicious events on a timeline, attaches webcam snapshots, and lets you set a verdict: **Clear**, **Flag for Review**, or **Escalate**. Summaries are powered by Claude Haiku and explain what was detected in plain language.

Students need no Proctorly account and nothing to install. They open the unique exam URL you share, grant camera and screen-share permissions through a guided setup flow, and take the test. Everything runs on standard browser APIs available in modern desktop browsers.
